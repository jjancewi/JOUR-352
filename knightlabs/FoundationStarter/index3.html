The Two Arenas of Steph Curry's Career

This page shows the evolution of Steph Curry and the Golden State Warriors since the start of the dynastic run in 2014. The Warriors had played in Oracle Arena for many decades prior to the start of the dynasty. However, the new ownership group planned to move the team across the bay from Oakland to San Francisco. Prior to the move, the Warriors and Curry saw the bulk of their dynastic success. The move to the Chase Center was jarring for some and brought on tough times. However, some Steph Curry brilliance brought some magic to the Warriors reminiscent of the days in Oracle.
